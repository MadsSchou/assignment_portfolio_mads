Responsive design

Solution description

There are multiple approaches to achieving website responsiveness. For instance, on my website, I utilized flexbox to position content within the menu in the most adaptable way possible, while grid was used in the first section to enable text to flow seamlessly through a column and an image to fill the second column.

The solution

Details

My assumption is that you frequently gaze at screens, including those of mobile devices, televisions, and computers. Due to the plethora of screen types, content must be flexible enough to cater to users' preferences and ensure an optimal experience across all platforms. Creating a responsive website entails several approaches, with my preference being "mobile-first," wherein you design the site based on the mobile screen and progressively develop it for larger screens such as tablets and computers. A superior adaptable content translates into a better user experience, a critical factor in today's crowded digital space.
